About this website

Ktravel

Since I was a child, I was very interested in searching the Internet. I jumped from one website to another. When I grew up, I still had the same interest as a child. But with the difference that this time I wanted to be the one who builds websites. I started with a lot of enthusiasm. I learned the necessary skills in order and started doing projects. I succeeded much faster than I thought. Building a website. I worked with teams. Participated in seminars and events. And I lived like this. One day after doing a long and difficult project. When I handed over the project, I went to bed and lay down. Suddenly, childhood memories came to my mind. Jumping from one website to another. I decided to go back to my computer again and reminisce. I did all this and I really enjoyed it. I decided to go to travel websites. Since I was very interested in traveling, I suddenly had an interesting thought. I started a travel website myself. And this is where ktravel was born.
